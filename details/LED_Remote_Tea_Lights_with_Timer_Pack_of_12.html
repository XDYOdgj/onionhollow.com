Modern Luminescence: LED Tea Lights for Any Occasion

These LED remote tea lights bring a contemporary flair to your lighting needs, replacing traditional candles with a safe and stylish alternative. With a simple click of the remote control, you can effortlessly manage the lights, eliminating the need for manual handling. Whether you're enhancing your living room, setting a romantic mood at a dinner party, or adding flair to your wedding decor, these lights are designed to deliver both beauty and convenience.

The pack includes 12 lights, giving you plenty to create the perfect ambiance. The realistic flickering amber flame enhances the visual appeal, making your space feel warm and inviting. Plus, with three timer options—4, 6, or 8 hours—you can set the lights to automatically shut off, saving energy and ensuring safety.

Unlike traditional candles, these flameless votive lights are completely safe for kids and pets, with no open flame or risk of fire. An excellent addition to any home decor, they are ideal for use in rooms, tables, or even outdoor spaces. Whether you're looking for practicality or a touch of elegance, these lights are built for both style and function.

  • Three timer options: 4, 6, or 8 hours
